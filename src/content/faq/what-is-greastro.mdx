title: "How are legal fees calculated?"
description: "Fee structure depends on the type of matter, its complexity, and the scope of the work involved."
order: 2
category: "Fees"
---
Legal fees vary depending on the services required. Some matters are best handled with a flat fee, while others may call for hourly billing or another tailored arrangement based on the anticipated scope of work.

We discuss fees as clearly as possible at the outset so clients understand what is included, what may change the scope, and how billing will be handled throughout the matter.
