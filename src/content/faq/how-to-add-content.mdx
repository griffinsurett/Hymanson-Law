---
title: "What should I bring to my first consultation?"
description: "Bring the documents and background details that will help us understand your matter quickly and accurately."
order: 1
category: "Consultations"
---
For an initial consultation, it is helpful to bring any wills, trusts, deeds, contracts, court papers, financial summaries, or correspondence that relate to your matter. A short timeline of key facts and a list of questions you want answered can also make the meeting much more productive.

If you are not sure what is relevant, bring what you have. We can review the materials together and identify what additional information, if any, will be needed.
