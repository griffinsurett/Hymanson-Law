## New Jersey and New York Business Law Attorneys

We provide comprehensive business law services. Tax consequences, wealth transfer, and general liability concerns play an important role in choosing the proper business entity and in determining how that entity should operate. When purchasing, starting, or operating a business, we assist clients in selecting the appropriate structure, including corporations, general partnerships, limited partnerships, limited liability companies, and joint ventures.

We also help clients evaluate the tax and liability issues that affect the formation and continued operation of a business. For closely held businesses, succession planning and exit strategies are especially important. We guide clients through the sale or transfer of a business to a third party or the next generation while seeking to minimize taxes and personal liability.
