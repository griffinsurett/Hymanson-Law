As part of our real estate services, we help determine whether property should be owned personally or through an entity.

We also establish the appropriate entity to own the real estate, such as a limited liability company, limited partnership, corporation, or trust. The entity chosen should address tax needs while also helping limit individual personal liability.

## Real Estate Practice Areas

Our work includes real estate matters throughout New Jersey and New York. Representative services and focus areas include:

- Residential and commercial purchases and sales
- Commercial leasing
- Developer representation
- Financing
- Lender representation
- Landlord tenant matters
- Construction associated with development
- Liens
- Foreclosures
- Boundary disputes
- Title claims
- Licensing issues
- Comprehensive real estate litigation
- Mixed use development

## New Jersey and New York Real Estate Attorney

Hymanson Law handles commercial and residential real estate matters of many kinds. Whether you are selling, purchasing, exchanging, or leasing property, we work to ensure your interests are protected throughout the transaction.

If you need legal advice before you purchase, sell, lease, or mortgage a property, we can help you evaluate the risks and structure the matter properly.
